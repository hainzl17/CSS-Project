Nintendo originally started out as a company selling Japanese playing cards in 1899.
But then in 1981, Shigeru Miyamoto came along and created Donkey Kong.
And then Nintendo really got going with video games when Miyamoto came up with Mario Bros in 1983,
as well as Super Mario Bros in 1985.
Miyamoto is also responsible for creating The Legend of Zelda in 1986.
